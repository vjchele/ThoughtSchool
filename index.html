About Us

We are a team with abundance hunger for learning in search of creative minds to spread the joy of learning.

Our Philosophy

With a strong desire to inspire and teach kids the beauty of Science, Math, and Computer Science, we would like to invite young, creative, and curious minds to come join us in this learning journey.

Our workshops are free and open for enthusiastic kids who would like to learn Computer programming, Robotics, Electronics, Math, and Science projects. However lot hardware based workshops would require students to buy specific programming kit.

Depending on the interest and age group of kids, we can conduct appropriate workshops covering topics such as Scratch Programming, LEGO programming, Arduino, Microcontrollers, Math etc.,

Our Free Workshops

Some of the workshops that we could organize based on the interest and age group of the participants

Scratch Programming

Scratch is a free programming language and online community where you can create your own interactive stories, games, and animations.

App Inventor

App Inventor lets you develop applications for Android phones using a web browser and either a connected phone or emulator.

LEGO Programming

Builders can program their STEM robots to talk, walk, grab, and more! Kids will love this creative and educational toy.

Note: You will need specific LEGO kit

Microcontroller Programming

A microcontroller is a "simple computer" that runs one program in a loop. There are several microcontrollers in the market such as Adafruit, BBC Micro.bit, Makey Makey etc., Programming these toys are fun.

Note: You will need specific microcontroller kit

Arduino Programming

Arduino is an open-source electronics platform based on easy-to-use hardware and software. Arduino uses a programming language (based on Wiring), and the Arduino Software (IDE), based on Processing.

Note: You will need a Arduino microcontroller kit

Python Programming

How will these Workshops work?
