    Learning Objectives

    1. Introduce learning mindset in students
    2. Enable students to learn and explore on their own
    3. Encourage students to communicate, share, and present their work
    4. Harbor team building
    5. Value and reward curiosity

    Materials Needed

    1. BBC microbit V2
    2. JST battery connector
    3. Micro USB Data cable
    4. Few LEDs, Resistors - as needed
    5. Servo motor - as needed
    6. Other interfacing hardware - as needed

    Show and Tell

    1. Demonstrate our learning and progress to friends and family
